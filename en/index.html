About Me — What I Can Do for You

Originally from Amsterdam, now settled in Värmland Sweden, I’ve spent many years working in construction, technical services, and IT. Over the years, I’ve collected a wide range of diplomas and certifications along the way…

Now based in beautiful Värmland, Sweden, I combine all those skills to help people with whatever needs fixing, installing, or improving — from practical household jobs to technical solutions. I also offer focused services like Home Check Värmland for people moving to Sweden and Vacation Home Management for owners who want reliable checks and upkeep while they are away.

As a multi-skill technician, I provide support for both practical home tasks and technical solutions. Whether you need installations, small repairs, or advanced custom PC builds, you can count on precision, flexibility, and reliable results every time.

I’m a hands-on problem solver who enjoys finding smart and practical solutions, both for homes and businesses. You can count on professional results, honest communication, and fair rates from 350-450 SEK/hour, depending on the task.

Based in Värmland, but always on the move across Sweden to help people get things done. Whether it’s hanging a lamp, fixing a leaky tap, assembling furniture, or handling small repairs around the house — I’m your go-to guy for practical everyday work. If you own a home in Sweden but are not always on-site, I can also provide regular checks and hands-on support through my vacation home management service.

I also take on larger projects such as renovations, installations, and troubleshooting — always with precision, care, and a no-nonsense attitude.

On the tech side, I’m passionate about building and repairing custom PCs — from silent, efficient workstations to high-performance liquid-cooled gaming rigs. Every build is tailored to your needs for top-tier performance and a clean, professional finish.
