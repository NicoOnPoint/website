What I can do for you

If you own a vacation home in Värmland and cannot be on-site all year, I can keep your property under regular supervision, provide clear updates, and support practical rental management.

During winter, I help protect the house with heating checks, frost-risk prevention, and practical actions to avoid expensive damage.

Both while you are away and during your stay, I can handle unexpected issues quickly and carry out practical adjustments, repairs, and renovation work when needed. I can also help manage rental operations, including stay preparation, cleaning, guest coordination, and post-stay follow-up.

When the property is rented out, I can support and assist guests throughout their stay. This includes coordinating cleaning, responding quickly to issues, and providing practical on-site help to make the stay as smooth and comfortable as possible.

  • Regular property checks (house + plot)
  • Winter routines: heating, frost prevention, safety checks
  • Small maintenance tasks and fast troubleshooting support
  • Cleaning before, between, and after stays
  • On-site coordination with technicians and service providers
  • Rental support: prep, check-in/out coordination, and follow-up
