What I can do for you

Do you want to buy a house in Sweden but cannot attend the viewing yourself? I can go on your behalf in Värmland. I create a video walkthrough and check the technical condition of the property so you get a clear and honest picture from a distance.

I keep close contact with you during the process, answer your questions directly, and help you move quickly when needed.

Beyond the home check itself, I also help you make the right choices and provide useful information before, during, and after your emigration. This includes practical points around location, local facilities, accessibility, planning, and realistic expectations for work after purchase.

What I check:

  • Roof and facade
  • Moisture issues and visible leak signs
  • Crawl space and basement (where accessible)
  • Electrical systems and installations (visible condition)
  • Windows, frames, and insulation impression
  • Likely renovation costs and risk points

After the viewing you receive:

  • Video of the property (inside and outside)
  • Photos of important details and attention points
  • Technical and practical advice
  • My honest impression of the property's condition
  • Extra decision support and practical emigration information tailored to your case
